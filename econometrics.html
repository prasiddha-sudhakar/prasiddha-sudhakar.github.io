What is Econometrics?

Application of statistics methods to economic data in order to give empirical content to economic relationships

Methods used:

  • Random controlled experiment: control and treatment group
  • Interference from observational data source
    • Cross Sectional: Key -> Value pairs
    • Time Series: Seeing trends over time
    • Panel: Multidimentional data obtained over several periods of time for the same firms/individuals
